Floats & Positioning

pull-left positions the element to the very left. pull-right positions the element to the right. 


                        
<div class="bg--grey-clear pull-left"></div>
<div class="bg--red pull-right"></div>
<div class="bg--blue pull-left"></div>
<div class="bg--yellow pull-right"></div>
